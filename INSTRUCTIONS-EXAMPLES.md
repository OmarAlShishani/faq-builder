# AI Instructions Examples

## What are AI Instructions?

AI Instructions are context or category information you provide to help the AI understand what domain or topic your FAQ questions belong to. This helps generate more accurate and relevant:
- **Tags** - Domain-specific categorization
- **Synonyms** - Terminology appropriate to your field
- **Responses** - Answers tailored to your industry

## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Writing Good Instructions

### DO âœ…
- **Be Specific**: Mention your exact industry and domain
- **List Topics**: Include main categories of questions
- **Define Tone**: Specify formal, casual, friendly, technical, etc.
- **Identify Audience**: Who will be reading these FAQs?
- **Mention Constraints**: Regulatory, compliance, or style requirements
- **Use Examples**: If specific terminology is important

### DON'T âŒ
- Be too vague (e.g., "answer questions")
- Contradict yourself
- Use overly complex language in instructions
- Include irrelevant information
- Leave out key context

## ğŸ¯ Structure Template

Use this template to write your instructions:

```
Domain: [Your industry/field]

Topics: [List main categories]

Tone: [Formal/Casual/Professional/Friendly/Technical]

Audience: [Who are your users?]

Special Requirements: [Any compliance, terminology, or style needs]

Additional Context: [Anything else that helps]
```

### Example Using Template:

```
Domain: Telecommunications company providing mobile and internet services

Topics: billing and payments, service plans, network coverage, technical support,
device troubleshooting, account management, new service activation

Tone: Professional yet friendly and helpful

Audience: Mobile and internet service subscribers (B2C and B2B)

Special Requirements: Use telecommunications terminology accurately.
Comply with consumer protection regulations. Provide clear escalation paths
for complex issues.

Additional Context: We have both prepaid and postpaid plans. Our coverage
includes 4G and 5G networks.
```
